OWASP MASWE

Mobile Application Security Weakness Enumeration
8 Categories · 99 Weaknesses · CWE-Mapped
⚠ Content in BETA — subject to change (mas.owasp.org)

Overview

MASWE adalah daftar security & privacy weaknesses yang umum ditemukan di mobile apps. Berfungsi sebagai bridge antara MASVS (requirements) dan MASTG (tests). Setiap weakness dipetakan ke CWE (MITRE) dan MASVS control.

  • Flow: MASVS Control → MASWE Weakness → MASTG Test → MASTG Demo
  • Profiles: L1 (baseline), L2 (enhanced), R (resilience), P (privacy)
  • Status: current (aktif), placeholder (draft), deprecated (usang)
  • MASTG v2.0.0: Released Jul 2026 — stable, non-beta. Legacy PDF deprecated.
  • Repo: github.com/OWASP/maswe
